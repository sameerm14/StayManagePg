Our Story

Founded in Sankeshwar, PG Comfort was born out of the need to modernize the traditional PG accommodation system. We understand the challenges faced by both PG owners and residents, and we're here to bridge that gap.

Our platform connects PG owners with potential tenants while providing tools to manage payments, maintenance requests, and communication - all in one place.
